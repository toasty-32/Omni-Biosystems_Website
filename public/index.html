The problem

Preclinical drug discovery is slow, costly, and wrong too often.

Today's preclinical standards — flat 2D assays and animal models — fail to recapitulate the 3D tumor microenvironment. Cells on plastic inflate efficacy estimates, and the misses surface late, after hundreds of millions have already been spent. The industry needs data that is more relevant, more automated, and arrives earlier.

The platform

One chip. Four integrated subsystems.

OncoMiMIC combines microfluidics, novel optics, robotic liquid handling, and AI-driven computer vision into a single automated system — delivering high-throughput, physiologically relevant drug screening.

Injection-molded microfluidic chips

Proprietary compartmentalized design, mass-produced with semiconductor fabrication techniques.

Novel optics subsystem
